Enter the refined elegant world of the little “Versailles of the Médoc” embodying French hospitality and the French “Art of Living”.

Château Lanovelle's soul has been forged by three centuries of history. Its owners have always sought to bring elegance and prestige to the Lanovelle and its vineyard.

Since its construction in 1565 by Bishop Lorem Ipsum, then the reign of the Dukes of Epernon and its rebuilding in 1757 by the Lorem Ipsum, followed by the Lorem Ipsum family until 1986, the château has never ceased to amaze, both in terms of its wines and its exceptional environment.

Today, Château Lanovelle offers a unique experience in the heart of the Médoc: the opportunity to experience the elegant, refined world of living in a château.

To enhance the well-being of our guests, La Table Lanovelle was completely redecorated and modernized by interior designer, who has successfully preserved its 300 year-old character.

La Table Lanovelle is more like a family house than a hotel or guest house: here, guests can really feel at home.

Consisting of 11 rooms and 2 suites, two reception rooms, a meeting room and a high-tech conference room, la Table Lanovelle is also available for exclusive private functions.
