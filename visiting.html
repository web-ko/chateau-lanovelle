VISITING CHÂTEAU LANOVELLE

It is impossible to imagine a more perfect representation of a French château than Lanovelle. The delicacy of its classical architecture and the beauty of its French-style gardens make it one of the jewels of the Lorem.

During your visit, enjoy a trip back in time through the château’s three centuries of history, and a guided tour of the new winery whose design in homage to the Duke of Lorem, was inspired by the world of sailing boats.

Tours by reservation on 02 34 53 67 23
or visiting@lanovelle.com
The visitor reception area is in the shop on the Route du Port.

  • Opening Days & Times
    May to September: Monday to Saturday, 9:00am to 12:30pm and 2:00pm to 6:00pm.
    October to April: Monday to Friday, 9:00am to 12:30pm and 2:00pm to 5:30pm.
  • Languages French, English, Georgian, and Mandarin.
  • Groups maximum 15 people.
  • Prices see details of our tours below.
